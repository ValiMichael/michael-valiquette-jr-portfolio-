Patent Portfolio

Innovative solutions recognized and protected by the United States Patent Office

US10624491B1

Vacuum Brewed Beverage Apparatus

Individual Patent Holder

A revolutionary vacuum-based cold brew coffee system that reduces traditional brew times from 12-24 hours to just 7-10 minutes. The apparatus utilizes carefully controlled vacuum pressure to accelerate extraction while maintaining the smooth, low-acid profile characteristic of cold brew coffee.

Key Innovations:

  • Precision vacuum control system for optimal extraction
  • Custom-designed brewing chamber with integrated filtration
  • Temperature-regulated process for consistent results
  • Food-safe materials and easy-clean design

Technical Achievement:

Combined principles of vacuum extraction, thermal dynamics, and fluid mechanics to create a consumer-friendly appliance. Required extensive prototyping, material testing, and process refinement.

Filed: 2019 Granted: 2020 Field: Food & Beverage Technology
US20240100608A1

Tool Holder & Parting and Grooving Tool for an Indexable Turret

Sandvik Coromant AB

An advanced tooling system designed to optimize modern CNC machining operations. This innovation addresses critical challenges in indexable turret applications, improving tool stability, repeatability, and overall machining efficiency.

Key Innovations:

  • Enhanced clamping mechanism for superior tool retention
  • Optimized geometry for reduced vibration and improved surface finish
  • Modular design allowing rapid tool changes
  • Improved chip evacuation for extended tool life

Technical Achievement:

Developed through extensive collaboration with manufacturing partners and rigorous testing in production environments. The design balances competing demands of rigidity, accessibility, and cost-effectiveness while delivering measurable improvements in machining performance.

Filed: 2023 Status: Published Application Field: Manufacturing & Tooling
